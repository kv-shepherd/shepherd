## Maintainer Responsibilities

Active maintainers are expected to:

1. **Review and merge pull requests** in a timely manner
2. **Triage issues** and ensure they have appropriate labels
3. **Participate in architectural decisions** (ADRs)
4. **Mentor new contributors**
5. **Ensure project quality and stability**
6. **Attend regular community meetings** (when established)

## Becoming a Maintainer

Contributors who meet the following criteria may be nominated:

1. **Sustained contributions** over at least 3 months
2. **Quality contributions**: Multiple merged PRs with good code quality
3. **Community engagement**: Helpful in issues, reviews, and discussions
4. **Technical understanding**: Familiarity with the project's architecture and ADRs

See [GOVERNANCE.md](GOVERNANCE.md) for the full maintainer nomination process.
